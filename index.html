About Cancer Diagnosis

An important process for early detection of the presence of cancer or reducing the risk of its spread. Early detection of cancer is vital to improve treatment chances and increase the chances of recovery. The type of screening used varies depending on the type of cancer and personal factors

Types of cancer diagnosis

There are many types of cancer screenings, which can be divided into two main groups: routine screenings and targeted screenings.

Lung cancer screening

Lung cancer is the most common type of cancer in the world, and smoking is the main cause of it. There is no routine screening for lung cancer, but there are some tests that can help detect cancer in its early stages, when it is most curable.

Brain cancer screening

There is no routine examination, but imaging, blood, or tissue tests may be used. The tests help determine the size and location of the tumor and the extent of its spread. Early diagnosis can help increase the chances of recovery.
